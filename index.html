Our Story

Made with Ammi's Hands

Tanveer's Take Out started the way all great things do — someone kept saying "you should sell this", and eventually Ammi listened. What began as feeding the family grew into feeding the neighbourhood, and then, anyone who could find the number.

Every recipe has lived in this family for decades. The spice ratios are memorised, not measured. The curry is never rushed. The cakes are built with the same focus most people reserve for engineering.

No shortcuts. No compromise on portions. Just the real thing — cooked fresh, packed with care, and delivered with love.
